This folder contains the three public group catalogues obtained in the UDS, UltraVISTA and VIDEO survey regions. 
Groups were detected with the DETECTIFz algorithm with the parameters detailed in DETECTIFz paper I.

These catalogues contain all groups detected with S/N > 1.5 in the range 0.1 < z < 3.15. 

We included several group properties such as position, redshift , S/N, R200, total_stellar_mass, richness. 
Please refer to the paper for more details on these quantities.


Header.

id - cluster identification number
ra - DETECTIFz right ascension (deg)
era -  uncertainty on right ascension (deg)
dec - DETECTIFz declination (deg)
edec -  uncertainty on declination (deg)
z - DETECTIFz redshift
zinf - minimum redshift of detection at SN>1.5
zsup - maximum redshift of detection at SN>1.5
log_dM - stellar mass density contrast in a circle of 250 kpc radius
SN - signal to noise ratio
R200c_Mass_median - median of R200 estimate
R200c_Mass_l68 - lower 68 confidence limit of R200 estimate
R200c_Mass_u68 - upper 68 confidence limit of R200 estimate
Ngal_M10.5_median - median of Ngal (richness) estimate
Ngal_M10.5_l68 - lower 68 confidence limit of Ngal (richness) estimate
Ngal_M10.5_u68 - upper 68 confidence limit of Ngal (richness) estimate
SMtot_M10.5_median - median of total stellar mass estimate
SMtot_M10.5_l68 - lower 68 confidence limit of total stellar mass estimate
SMtot_M10.5_u68 - upper 68 confidence limit of total stellar mass estimate
